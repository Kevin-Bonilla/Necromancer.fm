Necromancer.fm by Kevin Bonilla

iOS app dev project that I am working on to help me learn swift, swiftUI, and app dev

Necromancer.fm is a tool to help you rediscover or bring your music library back to life. The app connects to the music service of your choice and will make playlists for you to resurrect
your love for artists in your library that you may have forgotten about. It will keep track of which artists you've rediscovered, to give you progress of how much of your library you've 
revitalized.
